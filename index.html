🎤 Enable Microphone
The mic is blocked in the Claude viewer. Follow these steps to use voice input:
1
On this page, tap the ⋯ Share button at the bottom of Safari
2
Tap "Open in New Tab" — this gives the page a real URL with mic access
3
Tap the mic button 🎤 again — Safari will ask for permission, tap Allow
Already did that and still blocked?
Go to iPhone Settings → Safari → Microphone and make sure it's set to Allow.
Prefer to just type? The app works great without the mic — just type your question and options normally.
